About This App
How It Works
  1. Add your friends as participants
  2. Conduct your offline snake draft and record picks in the app
  3. The app automatically tracks player performance throughout the tournament
  4. Check the leaderboard to see who's winning
Scoring System

Scoring is simple - you get points for every point your drafted players score in the tournament. Once a player's team is eliminated, they stop earning points.

Data is updated automatically each night during the tournament.
